You're off on a wild outer space adventure! Somewhere in the galaxy Andromeda, floating among trillions of stars, you happen to run into another friendly COSMONAUT.

The Cosmonaut waves at you from his spaceship. "Friendly traveler!" he cries. "My spaceship is out of fuel! Can you help me?"

You explain to the cosmonaut that you would love to help, but you only have enough fuel to complete your own mission and get safely home.

"No, no," he says. "Allow me to introduce myself. My name is Mr. Fun Guy. I am the captain of a spaceship that runs on a very different fuel: magical space mushrooms! And they are floating around us everywhere amongst the stars! But they are very hard to catch, and I can't leave my ship! Can you help me?"

Help Mr. Fun Guy fuel up by catching him some magic mushrooms. Click on them as they appear while you fly through space. If you find a magic mushroom, you'll earn a fuel point. When you earn enough points, you'll fill up his tank.

"One more thing!" says Mr. Fun Guy. "NOT ALL OF THE MUSHROOMS ARE MAGIC! Some are even poisonous. Be careful with those! They'll destroy my spaceship's engine, and I won't be able to go anywhere."
